Display Bracket

  • This bracket is designed for DKD display and you can raise the unit for a better viewing angle with minor modifications.
  • Screen door handle brackets or windshield brackets can be used to relocate the display to a higher position.
  • Repurpose the handlebar clamps that hold the decorative 15mm tube above the handlebars. Remove the tube, then use 4" mending brackets to hold the display.

MetaMack

  • Controller upgrade to dramatically improve the performance of the Metacycle.

Windshield / Fairing

  • Krator Motorcycle Windshield Fairing Kit requires minimal modifications.
  • Amazon sells a short and a taller windscreen that both fit fairly easily. The headlight bolts almost fit the brackets, some minor filing may be helpful.
